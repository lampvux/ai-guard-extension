Protection Features

Link Protection

Verify that search result links match their displayed URLs and detect suspicious patterns

What it detects:

  • Domain mismatches between displayed and actual URLs
  • URL shorteners that could hide malicious destinations
  • Suspicious patterns like IP addresses or punycode domains
  • Homograph attacks using lookalike characters

AI Content Detection

Identify potentially AI-generated content in search results

Detection indicators:

  • Common AI language patterns and phrases
  • Repetitive sentence structures
  • Overly formal or consistent tone
  • Lack of contractions and perfect grammar

About AI Guard

AI Guard helps you browse more safely by:

  • Protecting you from phishing and malware links in search results
  • Identifying AI-generated content that might be misleading
  • Providing real-time security indicators while you search
